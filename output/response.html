The capital of France is Paris.wikipedia+2​

Paris is the capital and largest city of France, with an estimated city population of 2,048,472 people as of January 2025. The city is located along the Seine River in the north-central part of the country, approximately 233 miles upstream from the river's mouth on the English Channel.britannica+1​

Paris has served as the capital of France continuously since its liberation in 1944, with historical precedents dating back to 508 under King Clovis. The only notable interruption was during World War II (1940-1944) when the capital was temporarily moved to Vichy during German occupation.adelphi+1​

The city is situated in the center of the Île-de-France region and occupies a central position in the rich agricultural region known as the Paris Basin. Paris is renowned worldwide as a major center of finance, diplomacy, commerce, culture, fashion, and gastronomy, earning it the nickname "City of Light" due to its role during the Age of Enlightenment.bbc+2​britannica
